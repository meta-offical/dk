Leveraging Machine Learning – Clustering Algorithms

Why Analyze Agricultural Markets?

The agricultural market is complex, with prices influenced by seasonality, weather, geography, and demand-supply dynamics. Machine learning, especially clustering algorithms, can uncover hidden patterns in crop prices, production rates, and regional trends — helping farmers, traders, and policymakers make informed decisions.

Clustering in Market Analysis

Clustering algorithms like K-Means, DBSCAN, and Hierarchical Clustering group similar data points together. In agricultural markets, this could mean clustering crops by price volatility, region, or production volume.

  • Identify regions with similar crop pricing behavior
  • Group crops based on seasonality patterns
  • Spot outliers or abnormal price spikes
